## Verdict

Exact-P remains alive as the heavy upstream moonshot, but only through exact
theorem hooks:

```text
1. compact C,D,K,orientation theorem with one accepted raw branch;
2. exact equal-weight 75-atom normalized-y theorem;
3. exact theta2/theta2^-1 divisor-additive payload with period-156 bridge;
4. theorem-legal KL primitive word or mixed C3 x C169 selector with orientation;
5. Sprang/Kronecker sparse specialization of the theta2 packet;
6. explicit reverse reconstruction from the unified support-156 target.
```

The finite side is rigid and useful, but it is not the arithmetic producer.
Generic normalized-y, KL, Sprang, ray-class, theta, distribution, or
period-value vocabulary should not change exact-P status unless it supplies
one of the hooks above. A unified H0/conductor-39 theorem hit should route to
post-theorem extraction; it is not automatically an exact-P recovery.
