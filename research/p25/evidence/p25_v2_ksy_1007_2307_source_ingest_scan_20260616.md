## Observed Structure

Positive structure:

- The paper normalizes the derivative Weierstrass `wp'` function by the
  Dedekind eta function.
- It constructs an elliptic curve attached to an imaginary quadratic field and
  generates ray class fields by adjoining torsion points of that curve.
- The key normalized `y` object has the form
  `g_(0,2/N)(tau) / g_(0,1/N)(tau)^4` and lives on an `X_{1,4}(N)` surface
  when `4 | N`.
- The paper explicitly relates the work to the Lang-Schertz program and to
  Siegel-Ramachandra invariants.

Negative first-pass result:

- No `507`, `Norm_156`, `Y_507`, `period-156`, `Hilbert-90`,
  `finite-field`, `additive identity`, or `divisor identity` hit.
- No `U_chi`, `V_bal`, `chi_39`, `chi_3`, `chi_13`, `C_3`, `C_169`, `H0`,
  `N=39`, or `X_1(39)` hit.

Exact-P boundary:

- The paper gives the normalized-y atom vocabulary and torsion/ray-class
  generation context.
- It does not emit the exact p25 75-atom finite product, mixed
  `C_3 x C_169` graph, equal weights, orientation, or period-156 bridge.
