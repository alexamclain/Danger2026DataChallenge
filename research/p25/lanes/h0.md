# H0

## Purpose

Track the first-pass H0 source-theorem lane and keep its closing shape compact.

## Current Claim

H0 is one of the two best first-pass moonshot targets. The source-legality side
is real: Koo-Shin 2010 and the exact H0 translate screens certify the relevant
H0 products as legal source objects. What is still missing is the closer:

```text
one exact H0 product
+ arithmetic source theorem
+ finite divisor/additive or value theorem
+ Hilbert-90 / period-156 boundary
```

Without that theorem, H0 remains a source-certified lane, not a submission
route.

## Open Blockers

- The H90 / period-156 boundary is still missing from the source-stage closer.
- Even a source-stage win still has to route through DANGER3 framing, X1(16)
  extraction, halving, and official verification.
