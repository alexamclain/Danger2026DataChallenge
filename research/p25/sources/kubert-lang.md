# Kubert-Lang

## Purpose

Capture the current KL boundary for the mixed exact-product route.

## Current Claim

Kubert-Lang remains live only as exact exponent/dependence machinery. The
readable theorem pages show useful modular-unit language, but not the exact
mixed `C_3 x C_169` selector or the raw equal-weight p25 product.

So KL is source vocabulary and possible exact product structure, not a direct
closer on its own.

## Decisive Evidence

- [Kubert-Lang visual theorem boundary](../evidence/p25_ksy_y_kubert_lang_visual_theorem_boundary_20260614.md)
  records the readable theorem pages and their limits.
- [Exact-P lane](../lanes/exact-p.md) carries the live exact product ask that KL
  would still need to satisfy.

## Open Blockers

- No exact exponent matrix tied to the p25 row-labeled packet is in hand.
- Generic modular-unit generation is too broad for the current certificate
  target.
