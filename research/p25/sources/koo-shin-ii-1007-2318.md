## Purpose

Keep the sequel paper in view without confusing it with the missing 2010 Math.

## Current Claim

`1007.2318` is useful context, but it is not the missing Koo-Shin 2010 Section
5 / Theorem 5.2 source.

It helps with citation chain, sequel context, and some Siegel-function
arithmetic boundaries. It does not replace the 2010 paper for the active H0 and
conductor-39 theorem asks.

The constructive-payload scan keeps the same verdict at packet level: the
local extract has normal-basis/ray-class/Siegel/generator context, but no
one-edge theorem, exact p25 product packet, or period-156 value payload.
